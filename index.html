The hardest moments usually happen alone.

Apps, feeds, and promotions are designed to show up when you are tired, stressed, bored, paid, lonely, or already tempted. Guardrail is built around one simple idea: secrecy feeds the loop, support interrupts it.

01

02

Manual by design. Human on purpose.

The current MVP does not secretly watch your browser or phone. You open Guardrail when you feel the pull, then activate your support plan before the impulse becomes a decision.

Trust matters when the topic is sensitive.

Guardrail should feel like a serious support system, not a spying tool. The early product is intentionally simple so users can understand exactly what happens.

A

You choose when to activate

The MVP is user-triggered. Future automated detection will be opt-in and clearly explained.

B

The real SMS pilot will invite your accountability partner before they receive support alerts.

C

No shame loop

The goal is interruption, accountability, and recovery support, not punishment or public exposure.

Questions people ask first.

The product is sensitive by nature, so the first version needs to be easy to understand before anyone trusts it with real support.

Is Guardrail therapy?

No. Guardrail is an accountability tool. It can support recovery, but it is not medical care, therapy, or crisis support.

Will it monitor me?

The MVP is manual. You activate support yourself. Any future browser or device detection should be opt-in and transparent.

What does my partner see?

They receive the focus area and the response you asked for, so they know how to help without guessing.

Who is this for?

People working on betting, explicit content, doomscrolling, spending, gaming, or another impulse they want help interrupting.
